## üí° Pro Tips & Best Practices

### 1. **Start with What You Know**
Don't feel pressured to complete all 15 categories at once!
- Start with categories you have data for
- Come back to others later
- Your progress is auto-saved

### 2. **Use Templates Smartly**
- Templates are based on typical businesses
- Adjust the numbers to match your actual spending/activity
- Delete rows that don't apply, add rows for specific items

### 3. **Don't Overthink Emission Factors**
- The system auto-loads factors from authoritative sources (EPA, DEFRA, IPCC)
- You don't need to research or calculate factors manually
- Focus on accurate activity data (spending, distance, quantities)

### 4. **Break It Into Sessions**
- Do 3-5 categories per session
- Take breaks between sessions
- Your work is saved automatically

### 5. **Use the Examples**
- Click "Quick Start Examples" buttons
- Adjust the amounts to match your business
- Much faster than starting from scratch

### 6. **Skip What Doesn't Apply**
Not every category applies to every business:
- **Manufacturing:** Categories 1-5, 7, 9 likely apply
- **Office/Services:** Categories 1, 6, 7 are most relevant
- **Retail:** Categories 1, 4, 7, 9, 11, 12 matter most

### 7. **Review Before Final Save**
- Click "View Results" to see summary
- Check categories with highest emissions
- Verify numbers make sense for your business size

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to complete all 15 categories?
**A:** No! Only complete categories relevant to your business. It's fine to leave some at zero.

### Q: What if I don't know exact amounts?
**A:** Use your best estimate. You can always come back and update later. Estimated data is better than no data!

### Q: How accurate are the auto-loaded emission factors?
**A:** Very accurate! Factors come from authoritative sources like EPA, DEFRA, and IPCC. They're updated regularly.

### Q: Can I save partial progress and come back later?
**A:** Yes! Auto-save runs every 30 seconds. Close the browser and return anytime - your data will be there.

### Q: What if my business doesn't fit a template?
**A:** No problem! Start from scratch or pick the closest template and heavily modify it.

### Q: Can I edit data after saving to emission records?
**A:** Yes. Go to "Emission Records" page and edit individual records like any other emission entry.

### Q: What currency should I use for spending?
**A:** USD is default. If using other currencies, convert to USD first, or we'll add multi-currency support soon!

### Q: How do I handle remote employees (commuting)?
**A:** For fully remote employees, enter zero or minimal commuting. For hybrid, estimate average days per week in office.

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Problem: Emission factors showing as 0.00
**Solution:**
- Make sure you've selected a sector/type from dropdown
- Check internet connection (factors load from database)
- Refresh the page

### Problem: Auto-save not working
**Solution:**
- Check browser console for errors
- Ensure localStorage is enabled in browser
- Try a different browser (Chrome/Edge/Firefox recommended)

### Problem: Data disappeared after refreshing
**Solution:**
- Check if you're logged in to the same account
- Verify you're in the same browser (data is browser-specific)
- Use "Save to Emission Records" to persist data permanently

### Problem: Template not loading
**Solution:**
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Check if templates exist in database (run seeder if needed)
